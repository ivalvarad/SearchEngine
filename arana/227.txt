<p><b>Portal</b> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Burke_County,_North_Dakota" title="Burke County, North Dakota">Burke County</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Dakota" title="North Dakota">North Dakota</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. The population was 126 at the <a href="/wiki/2010_United_States_Census" title="2010 United States Census">2010 census</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2010_Census_.28City.29_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2010_Census_.28City.29-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> Portal was founded in 1893.</p>
<p>Portal sits along the <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canadian</a> border and is a major <a href="/wiki/Port_of_entry" title="Port of entry">port of entry</a> for road and rail traffic. <a href="/wiki/North_Portal,_Saskatchewan" title="North Portal, Saskatchewan">North Portal, Saskatchewan</a> is just over the border north of Portal. It is one of three year-round, 24-hour ports in North Dakota (the others being <a href="/wiki/Dunseith,_North_Dakota" title="Dunseith, North Dakota">Dunseith</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pembina,_North_Dakota" title="Pembina, North Dakota">Pembina</a>). The town is known for its "international" golf course. The ninth hole is partially in the United States and partially over the border in Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">2010 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Portal,_North_Dakota&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2010 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup id="cite_ref-FactFinder_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FactFinder-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> of 2010, there were 126 people, 61 households, and 38 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 225.0 inhabitants per square mile (86.9/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 80 housing units at an average density of 142.9 per square mile (55.2/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the city was 96.0% <a href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">White</a>, 0.8% <a href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">African American</a>, 2.4% <a href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">Native American</a>, and 0.8% from two or more races. <a href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">Hispanic</a> or <a href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">Latino</a> of any race were 4.0% of the population.</p>
<p>There were 61 households of which 23.0% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 49.2% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 9.8% had a female householder with no husband present, 3.3% had a male householder with no wife present, and 37.7% were non-families. 31.1% of all households were made up of individuals and 8.2% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.07 and the average family size was 2.58.</p>
<p>The median age in the city was 46.6 years. 19% of residents were under the age of 18; 8.7% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 18.3% were from 25 to 44; 40.4% were from 45 to 64; and 13.5% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the city was 54.8% male and 45.2% female.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">2000 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Portal,_North_Dakota&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2000 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a> of 2000, there were 131 people, 63 households, and 43 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 232.8 people per square mile (90.3/km²). There were 98 housing units at an average density of 174.1 per square mile (67.6/km²). The racial makeup of the city was 99.24% <a href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">White</a> and 0.76% <a href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)" class="mw-redirect">Native American</a>.</p>
<p>There were 63 households out of which 19.0% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 63.5% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 3.2% had a female householder with no husband present, and 31.7% were non-families. 31.7% of all households were made up of individuals and 14.3% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.08 and the average family size was 2.58.</p>
<p>In the city the population was spread out with 17.6% under the age of 18, 1.5% from 18 to 24, 24.4% from 25 to 44, 38.9% from 45 to 64, and 17.6% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 48 years. For every 100 females there were 101.5 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 92.9 males.</p>
<p>The median income for a household in the city was $36,406, and the median income for a family was $48,929. Males had a median income of $37,000 versus $20,500 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the city was $18,949. There were 7.1% of families and 10.0% of the population living below the <a href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line" class="mw-redirect">poverty line</a>, including no under eighteens and 10.3% of those over 64.</p>
