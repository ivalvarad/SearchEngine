<p><b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Disambiguation" title="Wikipedia:Disambiguation">Disambiguation</a></b> in Wikipedia is the process of resolving the conflicts that occur when articles about two or more different topics could have the same "natural" page title. This <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Category" title="Wikipedia:Category" class="mw-redirect">category</a> contains <i>disambiguation pages</i>: non-article pages containing links to other Wikipedia articles and disambiguation pages.</p>
<p>Disambiguation pages are added to <strong class="selflink">Category:Disambiguation pages</strong> by placing the {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Disambiguation" title="Template:Disambiguation" class="mw-disambig">Disambiguation</a>}} template on the page itself. Other templates are available that place articles in more specific disambiguation subcategories. A page may also be <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Assessment" title="Wikipedia:Assessment" class="mw-redirect">assessed</a> as "disambiguation-class" by various WikiProjects by including "|class=Disambig" in a WikiProject template on the talk page of the disambiguation page. This places the talk page in a subcategory of <a href="/wiki/Category:Disambig-Class_articles" title="Category:Disambig-Class articles">Category:Disambig-Class articles</a>.</p>
