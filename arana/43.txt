<p><span style="font-size:16pt">Featured pictures in Wikipedia</span></p>
<p>This page highlights the finest images on Wikipedia. The <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Featured_picture_criteria" title="Wikipedia:Featured picture criteria">featured picture criteria</a> explain that featured pictures must be <a href="/wiki/Free_content" title="Free content">freely licensed</a> or in the <a href="/wiki/Public_domain" title="Public domain">public domain</a>, must be of a high technical quality, and must add significantly to at least one article on Wikipedia. There are currently <b>5,656</b> featured pictures, and they are categorized by the topic they represent. All featured pictures are also available in a <a href="/wiki/Category:Featured_pictures" title="Category:Featured pictures">category</a>.</p>
<p>If you wish to add a specific image to this page, please nominate it on <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Featured_picture_candidates" title="Wikipedia:Featured picture candidates">Wikipedia:Featured picture candidates</a>.</p>
<p>One featured picture is chosen as the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Picture_of_the_day" title="Wikipedia:Picture of the day">picture of the day</a>. You can include this box anywhere (e.g., your user page) by adding the text {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Pic_of_the_day" title="Template:Pic of the day">pic of the day</a>}} or {{<a href="/wiki/Template:POTD" title="Template:POTD">POTD</a>}} where you want the picture to be shown.</p>
<p>Featured pictures from all Wikimedia projects can be browsed <a href="//commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Featured_pictures_by_subject" class="extiw" title="commons:Category:Featured pictures by subject">by subject</a> or <a href="//commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Featured_pictures_by_country" class="extiw" title="commons:Category:Featured pictures by country">by country</a> on <a href="/wiki/Wikimedia_Commons" title="Wikimedia Commons">Wikimedia Commons</a>.</p>
