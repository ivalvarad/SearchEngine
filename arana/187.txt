<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Featured_content_in_Wikipedia">Featured content in Wikipedia</span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a href="/wiki/File:Cscr-featured.svg" class="image" title="The featured content star"><img alt="The featured content star" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e7/Cscr-featured.svg/40px-Cscr-featured.svg.png" width="40" height="38" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e7/Cscr-featured.svg/60px-Cscr-featured.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e7/Cscr-featured.svg/80px-Cscr-featured.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="462" data-file-height="438" /></a></div>
<p>Featured content represents the best that Wikipedia can offer. This includes the articles, pictures, and other contributions that showcase the polished result of the collaborative efforts that drive Wikipedia.</p>
<p>All featured content undergoes a thorough review process to ensure that it meets the highest standards, and can serve as the best example of our end goals.</p>
<p>A small bronze star (<a href="/wiki/File:Cscr-featured.svg" class="image" title="The featured content star"><img alt="The featured content star" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e7/Cscr-featured.svg/14px-Cscr-featured.svg.png" width="14" height="13" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e7/Cscr-featured.svg/21px-Cscr-featured.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e7/Cscr-featured.svg/28px-Cscr-featured.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="462" data-file-height="438" /></a>) in the top right corner of a page indicates that the content is featured. This page gives links to all of Wikipedia's featured content.</p>
