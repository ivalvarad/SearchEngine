				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p><b>Portal</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_community" title="Unincorporated community" class="mw-redirect">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sarpy_County,_Nebraska" title="Sarpy County, Nebraska">Sarpy County, Nebraska</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Portal,_Nebraska&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Portal was established on the railroad.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> A post office opened at Portal in 1887, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1898.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
