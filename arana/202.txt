<p>This is a tracking category for disambiguation pages. It enables us to use <code>{{PAGESINCATEGORY:All article disambiguation pages}}</code> to get the exact number of disambiguation pages in the main namespace.</p>
<p>Pages are put in this category by templates such as {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Disambiguation" title="Template:Disambiguation" class="mw-disambig">Disambiguation</a>}}, {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Airport_disambiguation" title="Template:Airport disambiguation" class="mw-disambig">Airport disambiguation</a>}} and {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Roadindex" title="Template:Roadindex" class="mw-redirect">Roadindex</a>}}. That is, templates built with the {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Dmbox" title="Template:Dmbox">dmbox</a>}} meta-template, which is doing the actual categorising.</p>
<p>This category only lists pages in main (article) space.</p>
