(Archives are grouped by month of Main page appearance<a href="/wiki/Template:DYK_archive_header" title="Template:DYK archive header">.</a>)</p>
<p>Tip: To find which archive contains the fact that appeared on Did You Know?, return to the article and click "What links here" to the left of the article. Then, in the dropdown menu provided for namespace, choose Wikipedia and click "Go". When you find "Wikipedia:Recent additions" and a number, click it and search for the article name.<br />
