			<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Ah-Muzen-Cab</h1>
									<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">

								<div id="contentSub"></div>
												<div id="jump-to-nav" class="mw-jump">

				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p><b>Ah-Muzen-Cab</b><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Pronunciation" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Pronunciation"><span title="This word should have a pronunciation transcription or recording.">pronunciation?</span></a></i>]</sup> is one of the <a href="/wiki/Maya_civilization" title="Maya civilization">Mayan</a> gods of <a href="/wiki/Bee" title="Bee">bees</a> and <a href="/wiki/Honey" title="Honey">honey</a>. He is possibly the same figure as "the Descending God" or "the Diving God" and is consistently depicted upside-down. The Temple of the Descending God is located in <a href="/wiki/Tulum" title="Tulum">Tulum</a>.</p>
<p>He makes an appearance as a playable God in the 2014 free-to-play <a href="/wiki/Multiplayer_online_battle_arena" title="Multiplayer online battle arena">Multiplayer online battle arena</a> video game <a href="/wiki/Smite_(video_game)" title="Smite (video game)">Smite</a> as part of the <a href="/wiki/Maya_civilization" title="Maya civilization">Maya</a> pantheon.</p>
