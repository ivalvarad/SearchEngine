<p><b>October 19</b> is the 292nd day of the year (293rd in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 73 days remaining until the end of the year.<br /></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=October_19&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/202_BC" title="202 BC">202 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/Second_Punic_War" title="Second Punic War">Second Punic War</a>: At the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Zama" title="Battle of Zama">Battle of Zama</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roman_Republic" title="Roman Republic">Roman</a> legions under <a href="/wiki/Scipio_Africanus" title="Scipio Africanus">Scipio Africanus</a> defeat <a href="/wiki/Hannibal_Barca" title="Hannibal Barca" class="mw-redirect">Hannibal Barca</a>, leader of the army defending <a href="/wiki/Carthage" title="Carthage">Carthage</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/439" title="439">439</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Vandals" title="Vandals">Vandals</a>, led by King <a href="/wiki/Gaiseric" title="Gaiseric" class="mw-redirect">Gaiseric</a>, take <a href="/wiki/Carthage" title="Carthage">Carthage</a> in North Africa.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1216" title="1216">1216</a> – <a href="/wiki/King_John_of_England" title="King John of England" class="mw-redirect">King John of England</a> dies at <a href="/wiki/Newark-on-Trent" title="Newark-on-Trent">Newark-on-Trent</a> and is succeeded by his nine-year-old son <a href="/wiki/Henry_III_of_England" title="Henry III of England">Henry</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1386" title="1386">1386</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Heidelberg_University" title="Heidelberg University">Universität Heidelberg</a> held its first lecture, making it the oldest German university.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1466" title="1466">1466</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Thirteen_Years%27_War_(1454%E2%80%9366)" title="Thirteen Years' War (1454–66)">Thirteen Years' War</a> ends with the <a href="/wiki/Second_Peace_of_Thorn_(1466)" title="Second Peace of Thorn (1466)">Second Treaty of Thorn</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1469" title="1469">1469</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_II_of_Aragon" title="Ferdinand II of Aragon">Ferdinand II of Aragon</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Isabella_I_of_Castile" title="Isabella I of Castile">Isabella I of Castile</a>, a marriage that paves the way to the unification of <a href="/wiki/Aragon" title="Aragon">Aragon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Crown_of_Castile" title="Crown of Castile">Castile</a> into a single country, Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1512" title="1512">1512</a> – <a href="/wiki/Martin_Luther" title="Martin Luther">Martin Luther</a> becomes a doctor of theology (<i>Doctor in Biblia</i>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1649" title="1649">1649</a> – <a href="/wiki/New_Ross" title="New Ross">New Ross town</a>, <a href="/wiki/County_Wexford" title="County Wexford">County Wexford</a>, Ireland, surrenders to <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Cromwell" title="Oliver Cromwell">Oliver Cromwell</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1781" title="1781">1781</a> – At <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Yorktown" title="Siege of Yorktown">Yorktown, Virginia</a>, representatives of British commander <a href="/wiki/Charles_Cornwallis,_1st_Marquess_Cornwallis" title="Charles Cornwallis, 1st Marquess Cornwallis">Lord Cornwallis</a> handed over Cornwallis' sword and formally surrendered to <a href="/wiki/George_Washington" title="George Washington">George Washington</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Jean-Baptiste_Donatien_de_Vimeur,_comte_de_Rochambeau" title="Jean-Baptiste Donatien de Vimeur, comte de Rochambeau">comte de Rochambeau</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1789" title="1789">1789</a> – Chief Justice <a href="/wiki/John_Jay" title="John Jay">John Jay</a> is sworn in as the first <a href="/wiki/Chief_Justice_of_the_United_States" title="Chief Justice of the United States">Chief Justice of the United States</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1805" title="1805">1805</a> – <a href="/wiki/Napoleonic_Wars" title="Napoleonic Wars">Napoleonic Wars</a>: Austrian <a href="/wiki/Karl_Mack_von_Leiberich" title="Karl Mack von Leiberich">General Mack</a> surrenders his army to the <a href="/wiki/Grande_Arm%C3%A9e" title="Grande Armée">Grande Armée</a> of <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon Bonaparte</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ulm" title="Battle of Ulm">Battle of Ulm</a>. Thirty thousand prisoners are captured and 10,000 casualties inflicted on the losers.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1812" title="1812">1812</a> – <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon Bonaparte</a> retreats from Moscow.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1813" title="1813">1813</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Leipzig" title="Battle of Leipzig">Battle of Leipzig</a> concludes, giving <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon Bonaparte</a> one of his worst defeats.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1822" title="1822">1822</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Parna%C3%ADba" title="Parnaíba">Parnaíba</a>; Simplício Dias da Silva, João Cândido de Deus e Silva and Domingos Dias declare the independent state of <a href="/wiki/Piau%C3%AD" title="Piauí">Piauí</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1864" title="1864">1864</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Cedar_Creek" title="Battle of Cedar Creek">Battle of Cedar Creek</a>: <a href="/wiki/Union_Army" title="Union Army">Union Army</a> under <a href="/wiki/Philip_Sheridan" title="Philip Sheridan">Philip Sheridan</a> destroys a Confederate Army under <a href="/wiki/Jubal_Early" title="Jubal Early">Jubal Early</a>.</li>
<li>1864 – <a href="/wiki/St._Albans_Raid" title="St. Albans Raid">St. Albans Raid</a>: <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> raiders launch an attack on <a href="/wiki/Saint_Albans,_Vermont" title="Saint Albans, Vermont" class="mw-redirect">Saint Albans, Vermont</a> from Canada.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1866" title="1866">1866</a> – <a href="/wiki/Venice" title="Venice">Venice</a>, Annexion of <a href="/wiki/Veneto" title="Veneto">Veneto</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mantua" title="Mantua">Mantua</a> to Italy, at Hotel Europa, Austria hands over Veneto to France, which hands it immediately over to Italy.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a> – <a href="/wiki/Max_Planck" title="Max Planck">Max Planck</a>, in his house at <a href="/wiki/Grunewald" title="Grunewald">Grunewald</a>, on the outskirts of Berlin, discovers the law of <a href="/wiki/Black-body_radiation" title="Black-body radiation">black-body radiation</a> (<a href="/wiki/Planck%27s_law" title="Planck's law">Planck's law</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1904" title="1904">1904</a> – <a href="/wiki/Polytechnic_University_of_the_Philippines" title="Polytechnic University of the Philippines">Polytechnic University of the Philippines</a> founded as Manila Business School through the superintendence of the American C.A. O'Reilley.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a> – <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> takes possession of <a href="/wiki/Tripoli" title="Tripoli">Tripoli</a>, <a href="/wiki/Libya" title="Libya">Libya</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1914" title="1914">1914</a> – The <a href="/wiki/First_Battle_of_Ypres" title="First Battle of Ypres">First Battle of Ypres</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Love_Field" title="Dallas Love Field">Love Field</a> in <a href="/wiki/Dallas" title="Dallas">Dallas</a> is opened.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1921" title="1921">1921</a> – Portuguese Prime Minister <a href="/wiki/Ant%C3%B3nio_Granjo" title="António Granjo">António Granjo</a> and other politicians are murdered in a <a href="/wiki/Lisbon" title="Lisbon">Lisbon</a> <a href="/wiki/Bloody_Night" title="Bloody Night">coup</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1922" title="1922">1922</a> – British <a href="/wiki/Conservative_Party_(UK)" title="Conservative Party (UK)">Conservative</a> <a href="/wiki/Member_of_Parliament" title="Member of Parliament" class="mw-redirect">MPs</a> meeting at the <a href="/wiki/Carlton_Club_meeting" title="Carlton Club meeting">Carlton Club</a> vote to break off the <a href="/wiki/Lloyd_George_ministry" title="Lloyd George ministry">Coalition Government</a> with <a href="/wiki/David_Lloyd_George" title="David Lloyd George">David Lloyd George</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Liberal_Party_(UK)" title="Liberal Party (UK)">Liberal Party</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1933" title="1933">1933</a> – <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> withdraws from the <a href="/wiki/League_of_Nations" title="League of Nations">League of Nations</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1935" title="1935">1935</a> – The <a href="/wiki/League_of_Nations" title="League of Nations">League of Nations</a> places economic sanctions on <a href="/wiki/Fascist_Italy_(1922%E2%80%9343)" title="Fascist Italy (1922–43)" class="mw-redirect">fascist Italy</a> for its invasion of <a href="/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a> – The cargo vessel <a href="/wiki/MS_Sinfra" title="MS Sinfra"><i>Sinfra</i></a> is attacked by Allied aircraft at <a href="/wiki/Souda_Bay" title="Souda Bay">Souda Bay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Crete" title="Crete">Crete</a>, and sunk. Two thousand ninety-eight Italian prisoners of war drown with it.</li>
