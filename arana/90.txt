<p><b>October 20</b> is the 293rd day of the year (294th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 72 days remaining until the end of the year.<br /></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=October_20&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1548" title="1548">1548</a> – The city of Nuestra Señora de <a href="/wiki/La_Paz" title="La Paz">La Paz</a> (<a href="/wiki/Blessed_Virgin_Mary" title="Blessed Virgin Mary" class="mw-redirect">Our Lady</a> of Peace) is founded by <a href="/wiki/Alonso_de_Mendoza" title="Alonso de Mendoza">Alonso de Mendoza</a> by appointment of the king of Spain and <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Holy Roman Emperor">Holy Roman Emperor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_V,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor">Charles V</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1572" title="1572">1572</a> – <a href="/wiki/Relief_of_Goes" title="Relief of Goes">Relief of Goes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Crist%C3%B3bal_de_Mondrag%C3%B3n" title="Cristóbal de Mondragón">Cristóbal de Mondragón</a> with 3000 soldiers of the Spanish Tercios, release the siege of the city.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1720" title="1720">1720</a> – Caribbean pirate <a href="/wiki/Calico_Jack" title="Calico Jack">Calico Jack</a> is <a href="/wiki/Capture_of_the_William" title="Capture of the William">captured</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">Royal Navy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1740" title="1740">1740</a> – <a href="/wiki/Maria_Theresa" title="Maria Theresa">Maria Theresa</a> takes the throne of <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Austria</a>. <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">France</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Prussia" title="Kingdom of Prussia">Prussia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Electorate_of_Bavaria" title="Electorate of Bavaria">Bavaria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Electorate_of_Saxony" title="Electorate of Saxony">Saxony</a> refuse to honour the <a href="/wiki/Pragmatic_Sanction_of_1713" title="Pragmatic Sanction of 1713">Pragmatic Sanction</a> and the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Austrian_Succession" title="War of the Austrian Succession">War of the Austrian Succession</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1781" title="1781">1781</a> – <a href="/wiki/Patent_of_Toleration" title="Patent of Toleration">Patent of Toleration</a>, providing limited <a href="/wiki/Freedom_of_religion" title="Freedom of religion">freedom of worship</a>, is approved in <a href="/wiki/Habsburg_Monarchy" title="Habsburg Monarchy">Habsburg Monarchy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1803" title="1803">1803</a> – The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Senate" title="United States Senate">United States Senate</a> ratifies the <a href="/wiki/Louisiana_Purchase" title="Louisiana Purchase">Louisiana Purchase</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1805" title="1805">1805</a> – <a href="/wiki/Karl_Mack_von_Leiberich" title="Karl Mack von Leiberich">General Mack</a>'s army surrender to <a href="/wiki/Napoleon_I" title="Napoleon I" class="mw-redirect">Napoleon I</a> at <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ulm" title="Battle of Ulm">Ulm</a> after a few skirmishes.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1818" title="1818">1818</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_1818" title="Treaty of 1818">The Convention of 1818</a> signed between the United States and the United Kingdom which, among other things, settles the <a href="/wiki/Canada%E2%80%93United_States_border" title="Canada–United States border">Canada–United States border</a> on the <a href="/wiki/49th_parallel_north" title="49th parallel north">49th parallel</a> for most of its length.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1827" title="1827">1827</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Navarino" title="Battle of Navarino">Battle of Navarino</a>: a combined <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Turkish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Khedivate_of_Egypt" title="Khedivate of Egypt">Egyptian</a> armada is defeated by British, <a href="/wiki/Bourbon_Restoration" title="Bourbon Restoration">French</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Russian_Empire" title="Russian Empire">Russian</a> naval force in the port of Navarino in Pylos, Greece.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1873" title="1873">1873</a> – <a href="/wiki/Yale_University" title="Yale University">Yale</a>, <a href="/wiki/Princeton_University" title="Princeton University">Princeton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Columbia_University" title="Columbia University">Columbia</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rutgers_University" title="Rutgers University">Rutgers</a> universities draft the first code of <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> rules.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1883" title="1883">1883</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">Chile</a> sign the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Anc%C3%B3n" title="Treaty of Ancón">Treaty of Ancón</a>, by which the <a href="/wiki/Tarapac%C3%A1_Region" title="Tarapacá Region">Tarapacá</a> province is ceded to the latter, bringing an end to Peru's involvement in the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Pacific" title="War of the Pacific">War of the Pacific</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1904" title="1904">1904</a> – Chile and Bolivia sign the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Peace_and_Friendship_(1904)" title="Treaty of Peace and Friendship (1904)">Treaty of Peace and Friendship</a>, delimiting the border between the two countries.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1910" title="1910">1910</a> – The hull of the <a href="/wiki/RMS_Olympic" title="RMS Olympic">RMS&#160;<i>Olympic</i></a>, sister-ship to the ill-fated <a href="/wiki/RMS_Titanic" title="RMS Titanic">RMS&#160;<i>Titanic</i></a>, is launched from the <a href="/wiki/Harland_and_Wolff" title="Harland and Wolff">Harland and Wolff</a> <a href="/wiki/Shipyard" title="Shipyard">shipyard</a> in <a href="/wiki/Belfast" title="Belfast">Belfast</a>, <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland" title="Northern Ireland">Northern Ireland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1935" title="1935">1935</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Long_March" title="Long March">Long March</a>, a mammoth retreat undertaken by the armed forces of the <a href="/wiki/Communist_Party_of_China" title="Communist Party of China">Chinese Communist Party</a> a year prior, ends.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1939" title="1939">1939</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Pius_XII" title="Pope Pius XII">Pope Pius XII</a> publishes his first major <a href="/wiki/Encyclical" title="Encyclical">encyclical</a> entitled <i><a href="/wiki/Summi_Pontificatus" title="Summi Pontificatus">Summi Pontificatus</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1941" title="1941">1941</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: Thousands of civilians in <a href="/wiki/Kragujevac" title="Kragujevac">Kragujevac</a> in <a href="/wiki/Territory_of_the_Military_Commander_in_Serbia" title="Territory of the Military Commander in Serbia">German-occupied Serbia</a> are murdered in the <a href="/wiki/Kragujevac_massacre" title="Kragujevac massacre">Kragujevac massacre</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1944" title="1944">1944</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Army" title="Soviet Army">Soviet Army</a> and <a href="/wiki/Yugoslav_Partisans" title="Yugoslav Partisans">Yugoslav Partisans</a> liberate <a href="/wiki/Belgrade" title="Belgrade">Belgrade</a>, the capital of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Yugoslavia" title="Kingdom of Yugoslavia">Yugoslavia</a></li>
<li>1944 – <a href="/wiki/Liquified_natural_gas" title="Liquified natural gas" class="mw-redirect">Liquified natural gas</a> leaks from storage tanks in <a href="/wiki/Cleveland" title="Cleveland">Cleveland</a>, then <a href="/wiki/Cleveland_East_Ohio_Gas_explosion" title="Cleveland East Ohio Gas explosion">explodes</a>; the explosion and resulting fire level 30 blocks and kill 130.</li>
<li>1944 – American general <a href="/wiki/Douglas_MacArthur" title="Douglas MacArthur">Douglas MacArthur</a> fulfills his promise to return to the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a> when he commands <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Leyte" title="Battle of Leyte">an Allied assault</a> on the islands, reclaiming them from the Japanese during the Second World War.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1946" title="1946">1946</a> – Government of the <a href="/wiki/North_Vietnam" title="North Vietnam">Democratic Republic of Vietnam</a> decides that October 20 is Vietnam Women's Day.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1947" title="1947">1947</a> – The <a href="/wiki/House_Un-American_Activities_Committee" title="House Un-American Activities Committee">House Un-American Activities Committee</a> begins its investigation into <a href="/wiki/Communism" title="Communism">Communist</a> infiltration of <a href="/wiki/Hollywood" title="Hollywood">Hollywood</a>, resulting in <a href="/wiki/Hollywood_blacklist" title="Hollywood blacklist">a blacklist</a> that prevents some from working in the industry for years.</li>
<li>1947 – <a href="/wiki/Pakistan%E2%80%93United_States_relations" title="Pakistan–United States relations">United States of America and Islamic Republic of Pakistan</a> establish <a href="/wiki/Diplomatic_relations" title="Diplomatic relations" class="mw-redirect">diplomatic relations</a> for the first time.</li>
