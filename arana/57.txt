<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_1" title="October 1">October 1</a></b>: <b><a href="/wiki/National_Day_of_the_People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="National Day of the People's Republic of China">National Day</a></b> in China (<a href="/wiki/1949" title="1949">1949</a>); <a href="/wiki/Unification_Day_(Cameroon)" title="Unification Day (Cameroon)">Unification Day</a> in <b><a href="/wiki/Cameroon" title="Cameroon">Cameroon</a></b> (<a href="/wiki/1961" title="1961">1961</a>); <a href="/wiki/List_of_national_independence_days" title="List of national independence days">Independence Day</a> in <b><a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigeria</a></b> (<a href="/wiki/1960" title="1960">1960</a>), <b><a href="/wiki/Palau" title="Palau">Palau</a></b> (<a href="/wiki/1994" title="1994">1994</a>), and <b><a href="/wiki/Tuvalu" title="Tuvalu">Tuvalu</a></b> (<a href="/wiki/1978" title="1978">1978</a>); <b><a href="/wiki/Filipino_American_History_Month" title="Filipino American History Month">Filipino American History Month</a></b> begins in the United States</p>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1800" title="1800">1800</a> – With the signing of the <b><a href="/wiki/Third_Treaty_of_San_Ildefonso" title="Third Treaty of San Ildefonso">Third Treaty of San Ildefonso</a></b>, Spain returned the <a href="/wiki/Louisiana_(New_Spain)" title="Louisiana (New Spain)">colonial territory of Louisiana</a> to France in return for the <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a> area of Italy.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1890" title="1890">1890</a> – At the urging of preservationist <a href="/wiki/John_Muir" title="John Muir">John Muir</a> and writer <a href="/wiki/Robert_Underwood_Johnson" title="Robert Underwood Johnson">Robert Underwood Johnson</a>, the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">United States Congress</a> established <b><a href="/wiki/Yosemite_National_Park" title="Yosemite National Park">Yosemite National Park</a></b> <i>(<a href="/wiki/Yosemite_Valley" title="Yosemite Valley">Yosemite Valley</a> pictured)</i> in <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a> – The first section of the <b><a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania_Turnpike" title="Pennsylvania Turnpike">Pennsylvania Turnpike</a></b>, the United States' first inter-city <a href="/wiki/Controlled-access_highway" title="Controlled-access highway">expressway</a> comparable to the German <a href="/wiki/Autobahn" title="Autobahn">Autobahn</a>, opened to traffic.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> – <a href="/wiki/Croatian_War_of_Independence" title="Croatian War of Independence">Croatian War of Independence</a>: <a href="/wiki/Yugoslav_National_Army" title="Yugoslav National Army" class="mw-redirect">Yugoslav National Army</a> forces invaded the area surrounding <a href="/wiki/Dubrovnik" title="Dubrovnik">Dubrovnik</a>, Croatia, beginning a <b><a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Dubrovnik" title="Siege of Dubrovnik">seven-month siege</a></b> of the city.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/2005" title="2005">2005</a> – Terrorist suicide bombs <b><a href="/wiki/2005_Bali_bombings" title="2005 Bali bombings">exploded</a></b> at two sites in <a href="/wiki/Bali" title="Bali">Bali</a>, Indonesia, killing twenty people and injuring over 120 others.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1470" title="1470">1470</a> – With King <a href="/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England" title="Edward IV of England">Edward IV of England</a> forced to flee to the Burgundian Netherlands after a rebellion organised by <b><a href="/wiki/Richard_Neville,_16th_Earl_of_Warwick" title="Richard Neville, 16th Earl of Warwick">Richard Neville, 16th Earl of Warwick</a></b>, <a href="/wiki/Henry_VI_of_England" title="Henry VI of England"><span class="nowrap">Henry VI</span></a> was restored to the throne of England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1835" title="1835">1835</a> – Mexican <a href="/wiki/Dragoon" title="Dragoon">dragoons</a> dispatched to disarm settlers at <a href="/wiki/Gonzales,_Texas" title="Gonzales, Texas">Gonzales</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mexican_Texas" title="Mexican Texas">Texas</a>, encountered stiff resistance from a <a href="/wiki/Texian" title="Texian" class="mw-redirect">Texian</a> militia in the <b><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Gonzales" title="Battle of Gonzales">Battle of Gonzales</a></b>, the first armed engagement of the <a href="/wiki/Texas_Revolution" title="Texas Revolution">Texas Revolution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1937" title="1937">1937</a> – Under the orders of President <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Trujillo" title="Rafael Trujillo">Rafael Trujillo</a> <i>(pictured)</i>, Dominican troops <b><a href="/wiki/Parsley_Massacre" title="Parsley Massacre">began mass killings</a></b> of Haitians living in the Dominican Republic.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1968" title="1968">1968</a> – A peaceful student demonstration in the <a href="/wiki/Tlatelolco,_Mexico_City" title="Tlatelolco, Mexico City">Tlatelolco</a> section of <a href="/wiki/Mexico_City" title="Mexico City">Mexico City</a> <b><a href="/wiki/Tlatelolco_massacre" title="Tlatelolco massacre">was violently suppressed</a></b> when army and police forces fired into the crowd.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a> – A <a href="/wiki/Aircraft_hijacking" title="Aircraft hijacking">hijacked</a> airliner <b><a href="/wiki/1990_Guangzhou_Baiyun_airport_collisions" title="1990 Guangzhou Baiyun airport collisions">collided</a></b> with two other planes while attempting to land at <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou_Baiyun_International_Airport" title="Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport">Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport</a> in China, resulting in a total 128 fatalities.</li>
</ul>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_3" title="October 3">October 3</a></b>: <b><a href="/wiki/Hoshana_Rabbah" title="Hoshana Rabbah">Hoshana Rabbah</a></b> begins at sunset (Judaism, 2015); <b><a href="/wiki/German_Unity_Day" title="German Unity Day">German Unity Day</a></b>; <b><a href="/wiki/National_Day" title="National Day">National Day</a></b> in Iraq (<a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a>); <b><a href="/wiki/Public_holidays_in_South_Korea" title="Public holidays in South Korea">National Foundation Day</a></b> in South Korea</p>

<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 90px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg" class="image" title="Allen Ginsberg"><img alt="Allen Ginsberg" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg/90px-Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg" width="90" height="136" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg/135px-Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg/180px-Allen_Ginsberg_1978.jpg 2x" data-file-width="284" data-file-height="430" /></a>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/2333_BC" title="2333 BC" class="mw-redirect">2333 BC</a> – According to <a href="/wiki/Korean_mythology" title="Korean mythology">Korean legend</a>, <b><a href="/wiki/Dangun" title="Dangun">Dangun</a></b>, the "grandson of heaven", established <a href="/wiki/Gojoseon" title="Gojoseon">Gojoseon</a>, the first Korean kingdom.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_I" title="World War I">World War I</a>: Following his armed forces' defeat by the <a href="/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_I" title="Allies of World War I">Allied Powers</a>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Bulgarian_monarchs" title="List of Bulgarian monarchs">Bulgarian Tsar</a> <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_I_of_Bulgaria" title="Ferdinand I of Bulgaria"><span class="nowrap">Ferdinand I</span></a> abdicated in favor of his son <b><a href="/wiki/Boris_III_of_Bulgaria" title="Boris III of Bulgaria"><span class="nowrap">Boris III</span></a></b>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1935" title="1935">1935</a> – Italian forces under General <a href="/wiki/Emilio_De_Bono" title="Emilio De Bono">Emilio De Bono</a> <b><a href="/wiki/De_Bono%27s_invasion_of_Abyssinia" title="De Bono's invasion of Abyssinia">invaded</a></b> <a href="/wiki/Ethiopian_Empire" title="Ethiopian Empire">Abyssinia</a> during the opening stages of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Italo-Ethiopian_War" title="Second Italo-Ethiopian War">Second Italo-Abyssinian War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1957" title="1957">1957</a> – A <a href="/wiki/Superior_Courts_of_California" title="Superior Courts of California" class="mw-redirect">California Superior Court</a> judge ruled that "<b><a href="/wiki/Howl" title="Howl">Howl</a></b>", a poem by <a href="/wiki/Allen_Ginsberg" title="Allen Ginsberg">Allen Ginsberg</a> <i>(pictured)</i>, was of "redeeming social importance" and thus not <a href="/wiki/Obscenity" title="Obscenity">obscene</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1981" title="1981">1981</a> – The <b><a href="/wiki/1981_Irish_hunger_strike" title="1981 Irish hunger strike">hunger strike</a></b> by <a href="/wiki/Provisional_Irish_Republican_Army" title="Provisional Irish Republican Army">Irish Republican Army</a> prisoners at the <a href="/wiki/HM_Prison_Maze" title="HM Prison Maze">Maze</a> jail in <a href="/wiki/Belfast" title="Belfast">Belfast</a> ended after seven months and ten deaths.</li>
</ul>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_4" title="October 4">October 4</a></b>: <a href="/wiki/Calendar_of_saints" title="Calendar of saints">Feast day</a> of <b><a href="/wiki/Francis_of_Assisi" title="Francis of Assisi">St. Francis of Assisi</a></b> (Catholicism); <b><a href="/wiki/Shemini_Atzeret" title="Shemini Atzeret">Shemini Atzeret</a></b> begins at sunset (Judaism, 2015)</p>
<div style="float:right;margin-left:0.5em;" id="mp-otd-img">
<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 100px;"><a href="/wiki/File:JusticeJamesWilson.jpg" class="image" title="James Wilson"><img alt="James Wilson" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/JusticeJamesWilson.jpg/100px-JusticeJamesWilson.jpg" width="100" height="132" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/JusticeJamesWilson.jpg/150px-JusticeJamesWilson.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/JusticeJamesWilson.jpg/200px-JusticeJamesWilson.jpg 2x" data-file-width="304" data-file-height="400" /></a>
<div class="thumbcaption" style="padding: 0.25em 0; word-wrap: break-word;">James Wilson</div>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1779" title="1779">1779</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolution" title="American Revolution">American Revolution</a>: <b><a href="/wiki/James_Wilson" title="James Wilson">James Wilson</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> and his colleagues were forced to defend themselves after a mob, angered by his successful legal defense of 23 people from exile, converged on his house, resulting in six deaths.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1876" title="1876">1876</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Texas_A%26M_University" title="Texas A&amp;M University">Texas A&amp;M University</a></b> opened as the first public institution of higher education in the U.S. state of Texas.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_I" title="World War I">First World War</a>: The British devastated the German defence in the <b><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Broodseinde" title="Battle of Broodseinde">Battle of Broodseinde</a></b>, which prompted a crisis among the German commanders and caused a severe loss of morale in the <a href="/wiki/4th_Army_(German_Empire)" title="4th Army (German Empire)">German Fourth Army</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1957" title="1957">1957</a> – <a href="/wiki/Soviet_space_program" title="Soviet space program">Soviet spacecraft</a> <i><b><a href="/wiki/Sputnik_1" title="Sputnik 1">Sputnik 1</a></b></i>, the first artificial satellite to orbit the Earth, was launched by an <a href="/wiki/R-7_Semyorka" title="R-7 Semyorka">R-7 rocket</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Baikonur_Cosmodrome" title="Baikonur Cosmodrome">Baikonur Cosmodrome</a> near <a href="/wiki/Tyuratam" title="Tyuratam">Tyuratam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kazakh_SSR" title="Kazakh SSR" class="mw-redirect">Kazakh SSR</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/2010" title="2010">2010</a> – The dam holding a <a href="/wiki/Waste_pond" title="Waste pond">waste reservoir</a> in western Hungary <b><a href="/wiki/Ajka_alumina_plant_accident" title="Ajka alumina plant accident">collapsed</a></b>, freeing 1&#160;million cubic metres (1,300,000&#160;cu&#160;yd) of <a href="/wiki/Red_mud" title="Red mud">red mud</a>, which flooded nearby communities and killed at least nine people.</li>
</ul>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_5" title="October 5">October 5</a></b>: <b><a href="/wiki/International_Day_of_No_Prostitution" title="International Day of No Prostitution">International Day of No Prostitution</a></b></p>
<div style="float:right;margin-left:0.5em;" id="mp-otd-img">

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1789" title="1789">1789</a> – <a href="/wiki/French_Revolution" title="French Revolution">French Revolution</a>: Upset about the high price and scarcity of bread, thousands of Parisian women and their various allies <b><a href="/wiki/Women%27s_March_on_Versailles" title="Women's March on Versailles">marched</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> on the royal palace at <a href="/wiki/Palace_of_Versailles" title="Palace of Versailles">Versailles</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1910" title="1910">1910</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_Republican_Party" title="Portuguese Republican Party">Portuguese Republican Party</a> organised <b><a href="/wiki/5_October_1910_revolution" title="5 October 1910 revolution">a <i>coup d'etat</i></a></b>, deposed the <a href="/wiki/Constitutional_monarchy" title="Constitutional monarchy">constitutional monarchy</a> and implanted a <a href="/wiki/Republicanism" title="Republicanism">republican</a> regime in Portugal.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1930" title="1930">1930</a> – The British <a href="/wiki/Airship" title="Airship">airship</a> <b><a href="/wiki/R101" title="R101">R101</a></b> crashed in France en route to India on its maiden voyage, killing 48 passengers and crew.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a> – <a href="/wiki/Dirty_War" title="Dirty War">Dirty War</a>: The guerrilla group <a href="/wiki/Montoneros" title="Montoneros">Montoneros</a> carried out <i><b><a href="/wiki/Operation_Primicia" title="Operation Primicia">Operation Primicia</a></b></i>, a terrorist attack in which they hijacked an <a href="/wiki/Aerol%C3%ADneas_Argentinas" title="Aerolíneas Argentinas">Aerolíneas Argentinas</a> flight, captured the <a href="/wiki/Formosa_International_Airport" title="Formosa International Airport">Formosa International Airport</a>, and attacked a military regiment.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/2000" title="2000">2000</a> – Serbian engineering vehicle operator <a href="/wiki/Ljubisav_%C4%90oki%C4%87" title="Ljubisav Đokić">Ljubisav Đokić</a> rammed the <a href="/wiki/Radio_Television_of_Serbia" title="Radio Television of Serbia">Radio Television of Serbia</a> building with a <a href="/wiki/Wheel_loader" title="Wheel loader" class="mw-redirect">wheel loader</a> in a protest known as the <b><a href="/wiki/Overthrow_of_Slobodan_Milo%C5%A1evi%C4%87" title="Overthrow of Slobodan Milošević">Bulldozer Revolution</a></b>, causing <a href="/wiki/Slobodan_Milo%C5%A1evi%C4%87" title="Slobodan Milošević">Slobodan Milošević</a> to resign two days later.</li>
</ul>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_6" title="October 6">October 6</a></b>: <b><a href="/wiki/German-American_Day" title="German-American Day">German-American Day</a></b> in the United States</p>
<div style="float:right;margin-left:0.5em;" id="mp-otd-img">
<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 120px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg" class="image" title="Carlos Álvarez"><img alt="Carlos Álvarez" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d2/Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg/120px-Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg" width="120" height="91" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d2/Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg/180px-Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d2/Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg/240px-Carlos_%C3%81lvarez.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2251" data-file-height="1705" /></a>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1777" title="1777">1777</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: British forces under the command of General Sir <a href="/wiki/Henry_Clinton_(American_War_of_Independence)" title="Henry Clinton (American War of Independence)">Henry Clinton</a> <b><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Forts_Clinton_and_Montgomery" title="Battle of Forts Clinton and Montgomery">captured</a></b> <a href="/wiki/Fort_Clinton" title="Fort Clinton">Fort Clinton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fort_Montgomery_(Hudson_River)" title="Fort Montgomery (Hudson River)">Fort Montgomery</a>, and then dismantled the <a href="/wiki/Hudson_River_Chain" title="Hudson River Chain">Hudson River Chain</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1910" title="1910">1910</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Eleftherios_Venizelos" title="Eleftherios Venizelos">Eleftherios Venizelos</a></b> was elected <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Greece" title="Prime Minister of Greece">Prime Minister of Greece</a> for the first of his seven non-consecutive terms.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> – The first successful feature sound film <i><b><a href="/wiki/The_Jazz_Singer" title="The Jazz Singer">The Jazz Singer</a></b></i>, starring <a href="/wiki/Al_Jolson" title="Al Jolson">Al Jolson</a>, was released.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a> – Police constable <b><a href="/wiki/Death_of_Keith_Blakelock" title="Death of Keith Blakelock">Keith Blakelock</a></b> was killed during <a href="/wiki/Broadwater_Farm_riot" title="Broadwater Farm riot">rioting</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Broadwater_Farm" title="Broadwater Farm">Broadwater Farm</a> housing estate in <a href="/wiki/Tottenham" title="Tottenham">Tottenham</a>, London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/2000" title="2000">2000</a> – Denouncing corruption in the administration of <a href="/wiki/President_of_Argentina" title="President of Argentina">Argentine President</a> <a href="/wiki/Fernando_de_la_R%C3%BAa" title="Fernando de la Rúa">Fernando de la Rúa</a> and in the <a href="/wiki/Argentine_Senate" title="Argentine Senate">Senate</a>, Vice President <b><a href="/wiki/Carlos_%C3%81lvarez_(politician)" title="Carlos Álvarez (politician)">Carlos Álvarez</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> resigned from his office.</li>
</ul>

<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 100px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Hua_Guofeng-1.jpg" class="image" title="Hua Guofeng"><img alt="Hua Guofeng" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/09/Hua_Guofeng-1.jpg/100px-Hua_Guofeng-1.jpg" width="100" height="132" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/09/Hua_Guofeng-1.jpg/150px-Hua_Guofeng-1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/09/Hua_Guofeng-1.jpg 2x" data-file-width="182" data-file-height="241" /></a>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1542" title="1542">1542</a> – Spanish explorer <a href="/wiki/Juan_Rodr%C3%ADguez_Cabrillo" title="Juan Rodríguez Cabrillo">Juan Rodríguez Cabrillo</a> became the first European to set foot on <b><a href="/wiki/Santa_Catalina_Island,_California" title="Santa Catalina Island, California">Santa Catalina Island</a></b>, off the coast of <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1763" title="1763">1763</a> – Following Great Britain's acquisition of <a href="/wiki/New_France" title="New France">New France</a> after the end of the <a href="/wiki/Seven_Years%27_War" title="Seven Years' War">Seven Years' War</a>, King <a href="/wiki/George_III_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="George III of the United Kingdom"><span class="nowrap">George III</span></a> issued a <b><a href="/wiki/Royal_Proclamation_of_1763" title="Royal Proclamation of 1763">Royal Proclamation</a></b> closing most of this land to the residents of the <a href="/wiki/Thirteen_Colonies" title="Thirteen Colonies">Thirteen Colonies</a> and reserving it for <a href="/wiki/Indigenous_peoples_of_the_Americas" title="Indigenous peoples of the Americas">indigenous peoples</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/KLM" title="KLM">KLM</a></b>, the oldest airline in the world still operating under its original name, was founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1976" title="1976">1976</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Hua_Guofeng" title="Hua Guofeng">Hua Guofeng</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> succeeded <a href="/wiki/Mao_Zedong" title="Mao Zedong">Mao Zedong</a> as <a href="/wiki/Chairman_of_the_Communist_Party_of_China" title="Chairman of the Communist Party of China">Chairman of the Communist Party of China</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/2001" title="2001">2001</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_on_Terror" title="War on Terror">War on Terrorism</a>: The <b><a href="/wiki/War_in_Afghanistan_(2001%E2%80%93present)" title="War in Afghanistan (2001–present)" class="mw-redirect">War in Afghanistan</a></b> began with an aerial bombing campaign targeting <a href="/wiki/Taliban" title="Taliban">Taliban</a> and <a href="/wiki/Al-Qaeda" title="Al-Qaeda">Al-Qaeda</a> forces.</li>
</ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/1871" title="1871">1871</a> – Four large fires broke out in the United States, including the <b><a href="/wiki/Great_Chicago_Fire" title="Great Chicago Fire">Great Chicago Fire</a></b> and the <b><a href="/wiki/Peshtigo_Fire" title="Peshtigo Fire">Peshtigo Fire</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">Wisconsin</a>, the latter being the deadliest in U.S. history.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a> – The <b><a href="/wiki/Indian_Air_Force" title="Indian Air Force">Indian Air Force</a></b> was founded as an auxiliary air force of the <a href="/wiki/British_Raj" title="British Raj">Indian Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1962" title="1962">1962</a> – <a href="/wiki/Newsmagazine" title="Newsmagazine" class="mw-redirect">Newsmagazine</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Der_Spiegel" title="Der Spiegel">Der Spiegel</a></i> <b><a href="/wiki/Spiegel_scandal" title="Spiegel scandal">revealed</a></b> the unpreparedness of the <a href="/wiki/Bundeswehr" title="Bundeswehr">West German armed forces</a> against the communist threat from the east, and was accused of treason shortly afterwards.</li>

</ul>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_11" title="October 11">October 11</a></b>: <b><a href="/wiki/National_Coming_Out_Day" title="National Coming Out Day">National Coming Out Day</a></b> (international)</p>
<div style="float:right;margin-left:0.5em;" id="mp-otd-img">
<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 120px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Zwingli_cropped.JPG" class="image" title="Huldrych Zwingli"><img alt="Huldrych Zwingli" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Zwingli_cropped.JPG/120px-Zwingli_cropped.JPG" width="120" height="129" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Zwingli_cropped.JPG/180px-Zwingli_cropped.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Zwingli_cropped.JPG/240px-Zwingli_cropped.JPG 2x" data-file-width="598" data-file-height="644" /></a>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1492" title="1492">1492</a> – Members of the <a href="/wiki/Voyages_of_Christopher_Columbus#First_voyage" title="Voyages of Christopher Columbus">first voyage of Christopher Columbus</a> reported the <b><a href="/wiki/1492_light_sighting" title="1492 light sighting">sighting of unknown light</a></b> on their way to <a href="/wiki/Guanahani" title="Guanahani">Guanahani</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1531" title="1531">1531</a> – <a href="/wiki/Reformation_in_Switzerland" title="Reformation in Switzerland">Swiss Reformation</a> leader <b><a href="/wiki/Huldrych_Zwingli" title="Huldrych Zwingli">Huldrych Zwingli</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> was killed in battle when Catholic <a href="/wiki/Cantons_of_Switzerland" title="Cantons of Switzerland">cantons</a> <a href="/wiki/Second_War_of_Kappel" title="Second War of Kappel">attacked in response</a> to a food <a href="/wiki/Blockade" title="Blockade">blockade</a> being applied by his alliance.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1767" title="1767">1767</a> – Unable to go any further, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Mason" title="Charles Mason">Charles Mason</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jeremiah_Dixon" title="Jeremiah Dixon">Jeremiah Dixon</a> made their final observations for what would become known as the <b><a href="/wiki/Mason%E2%80%93Dixon_line" title="Mason–Dixon line">Mason–Dixon line</a></b>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1942" title="1942">1942</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: At the <b><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Cape_Esperance" title="Battle of Cape Esperance">Battle of Cape Esperance</a></b> on the northwest coast of <a href="/wiki/Guadalcanal" title="Guadalcanal">Guadalcanal</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States_Navy" title="United States Navy">American ships</a> intercepted and defeated a <a href="/wiki/Imperial_Japanese_Navy" title="Imperial Japanese Navy">Japanese fleet</a> on their way to destroy <a href="/wiki/Henderson_Field_(Guadalcanal)" title="Henderson Field (Guadalcanal)">Henderson Field</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1975" title="1975">1975</a> – <i><b><a href="/wiki/Saturday_Night_Live" title="Saturday Night Live">Saturday Night Live</a></b></i>, an American weekly <a href="/wiki/Sketch_comedy" title="Sketch comedy">sketch comedy</a>–<a href="/wiki/Variety_show" title="Variety show">variety show</a>, was broadcast for the first time.</li>
</ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/2005" title="2005">2005</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Wilma" title="Hurricane Wilma">Hurricane Wilma</a></b> became the most intense <a href="/wiki/North_Atlantic_tropical_cyclone" title="North Atlantic tropical cyclone" class="mw-redirect">Atlantic hurricane</a> on record with a minimum <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_pressure" title="Atmospheric pressure">atmospheric pressure</a> of <span class="nowrap">882 <a href="/wiki/Bar_(unit)" title="Bar (unit)">mbar</a></span>.</li>
</ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/1818" title="1818">1818</a> – The United Kingdom and the United States signed the <b><a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_1818" title="Treaty of 1818">Treaty of 1818</a></b>, which settled the <a href="/wiki/Canada%E2%80%93United_States_border" title="Canada–United States border">Canada–United States border</a> on the <a href="/wiki/49th_parallel_north" title="49th parallel north">49th parallel</a> between the Pacific Ocean and <a href="/wiki/Lake_of_the_Woods" title="Lake of the Woods">Lake of the Woods</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a> – During a <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Europa_League" title="UEFA Europa League">UEFA Cup</a> match between <a href="/wiki/FC_Spartak_Moscow" title="FC Spartak Moscow">FC Spartak Moscow</a> and <a href="/wiki/HFC_Haarlem" title="HFC Haarlem">HFC Haarlem</a>, a large number of attendees tried to leave the <a href="/wiki/Luzhniki_Stadium" title="Luzhniki Stadium">Grand Sports Arena of the Central Lenin Stadium</a> at the same time, resulting in <b><a href="/wiki/Luzhniki_disaster" title="Luzhniki disaster">a stampede</a></b> that caused 66 deaths.</li>

Guggenheim Museum</a></b>, designed by American architect <a href="/wiki/Frank_Lloyd_Wright" title="Frank Lloyd Wright">Frank Lloyd Wright</a>, opened in New York City.</li>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/October_24" title="October 24">October 24</a></b>: <b><a href="/wiki/Day_of_Ashura" title="Day of Ashura">Day of Ashura</a></b> (Islam, 2015); <b><a href="/wiki/United_Nations_Day" title="United Nations Day">United Nations Day</a></b> (<a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a>)</p>
<div style="float:right;margin-left:0.5em;" id="mp-otd-img">
<p><a href="/wiki/File:William_Lassell.jpg" class="image" title="William Lassell"><img alt="William Lassell" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fa/William_Lassell.jpg/66px-William_Lassell.jpg" width="66" height="100" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fa/William_Lassell.jpg/99px-William_Lassell.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fa/William_Lassell.jpg/133px-William_Lassell.jpg 2x" data-file-width="355" data-file-height="535" /></a></p>
</div>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1648" title="1648">1648</a> – The second treaty of the <b><a href="/wiki/Peace_of_Westphalia" title="Peace of Westphalia">Peace of Westphalia</a></b>, the Treaty of Münster, was signed, ending both the <a href="/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War" title="Thirty Years' War">Thirty Years' War</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_Revolt" title="Dutch Revolt">Dutch Revolt</a>, and officially recognizing the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_Republic" title="Dutch Republic">Republic of the Seven United Netherlands</a> and <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Swiss Confederation</a> as independent states.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1851" title="1851">1851</a> – <a href="/wiki/William_Lassell" title="William Lassell">William Lassell</a> <i>(pictured)</i> discovered the <a href="/wiki/Moons_of_Uranus" title="Moons of Uranus">Uranian moons</a> <b><a href="/wiki/Umbriel_(moon)" title="Umbriel (moon)">Umbriel</a></b> and <b><a href="/wiki/Ariel_(moon)" title="Ariel (moon)">Ariel</a></b>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a> – <a href="/wiki/First_Balkan_War" title="First Balkan War">First Balkan War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Serbia" title="Kingdom of Serbia">Serbian</a> forces defeated the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman</a> army at the <b><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Kumanovo" title="Battle of Kumanovo">Battle of Kumanovo</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Vardar_Macedonia" title="Vardar Macedonia">Vardar Macedonia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1944" title="1944">1944</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Imperial_Japanese_Navy" title="Imperial Japanese Navy">Imperial Japanese</a> battleship <i><b><a href="/wiki/Japanese_battleship_Musashi" title="Japanese battleship Musashi">Musashi</a></b></i>, one of the heaviest and most powerfully armed ever constructed, was sunk in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Leyte_Gulf" title="Battle of Leyte Gulf">Battle of Leyte Gulf</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1964" title="1964">1964</a> – The military court of <a href="/wiki/State_of_Vietnam" title="State of Vietnam">South Vietnamese junta</a> chief <a href="/wiki/Nguyen_Khanh" title="Nguyen Khanh" class="mw-redirect">Nguyen Khanh</a> acquitted Generals <b><a href="/wiki/D%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_V%C4%83n_%C4%90%E1%BB%A9c" title="Dương Văn Đức">Dương Văn Đức</a></b> and <b><a href="/wiki/L%C3%A2m_V%C4%83n_Ph%C3%A1t" title="Lâm Văn Phát">Lâm Văn Phát</a></b> of leading a <a href="/wiki/September_1964_South_Vietnamese_coup_attempt" title="September 1964 South Vietnamese coup attempt">coup attempt</a> against Khanh, despite the pair's proclamation of his overthrow during their military action.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1420" title="1420">1420</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a></b> was officially designated the capital of the <a href="/wiki/Ming_dynasty" title="Ming dynasty">Ming dynasty</a> in the same year that the <a href="/wiki/Forbidden_City" title="Forbidden City">Forbidden City</a> <i>(pictured)</i>, the seat of government, was completed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1835" title="1835">1835</a> – <a href="/wiki/M%C4%81ori_people" title="Māori people">Māori</a> chiefs signed the <b><a href="/wiki/Declaration_of_the_Independence_of_New_Zealand" title="Declaration of the Independence of New Zealand">Declaration of the Independence of New Zealand</a></b> and established the <b><a href="/wiki/United_Tribes_of_New_Zealand" title="United Tribes of New Zealand">United Tribes of New Zealand</a></b>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1891" title="1891">1891</a> – The <b><a href="/wiki/1891_Mino-Owari_earthquake" title="1891 Mino-Owari earthquake" class="mw-redirect">Nōbi Earthquake</a></b>, Japan's strongest known inland earthquake, struck the former provinces of <a href="/wiki/Mino_Province" title="Mino Province">Mino</a> and <a href="/wiki/Owari_Province" title="Owari Province">Owari</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">U.S. Congress</a> passed the <b><a href="/wiki/Volstead_Act" title="Volstead Act">Volstead Act</a></b> over President <a href="/wiki/Woodrow_Wilson" title="Woodrow Wilson">Woodrow Wilson</a>'s veto, reinforcing <a href="/wiki/Prohibition_in_the_United_States" title="Prohibition in the United States">Prohibition in the United States</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1995" title="1995">1995</a> – A <b><a href="/wiki/1995_Baku_Metro_fire" title="1995 Baku Metro fire">fire in Baku Metro</a></b>, Azerbaijan, killed at least 286 passengers and injured 270 more in the world's deadliest <a href="/wiki/Rapid_transit" title="Rapid transit">subway</a> disaster.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/539_BC" title="539 BC">539 BC</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Cyrus_the_Great" title="Cyrus the Great">Cyrus the Great</a></b> captured <a href="/wiki/Babylon" title="Babylon">Babylon</a>, incorporating the <a href="/wiki/Neo-Babylonian_Empire" title="Neo-Babylonian Empire">Neo-Babylonian Empire</a> and making the <a href="/wiki/Achaemenid_Empire" title="Achaemenid Empire">Achaemenid Empire</a> the largest in the history of the world.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a> – Lt. <a href="/wiki/William_Robert_Broughton" title="William Robert Broughton">William Broughton</a>, a member of Captain <a href="/wiki/George_Vancouver" title="George Vancouver">George Vancouver</a>'s discovery expedition, observed a peak in what is now <a href="/wiki/Oregon" title="Oregon">Oregon</a>, US, and named it <b><a href="/wiki/Mount_Hood" title="Mount Hood">Mount Hood</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> after British admiral <a href="/wiki/Samuel_Hood,_1st_Viscount_Hood" title="Samuel Hood, 1st Viscount Hood">Samuel Hood</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1929" title="1929">1929</a> – About 16&#160;million shares were traded on the <a href="/wiki/New_York_Stock_Exchange" title="New York Stock Exchange">New York Stock Exchange</a> on "<b><a href="/wiki/Wall_Street_Crash_of_1929" title="Wall Street Crash of 1929">Black Tuesday</a></b>", a record that stood for almost 40&#160;years, making a total of $30&#160;billion that had been lost over two days.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1998" title="1998">1998</a> – The <b><a href="/wiki/Truth_and_Reconciliation_Commission_(South_Africa)" title="Truth and Reconciliation Commission (South Africa)">Truth and Reconciliation Commission</a></b> presented its report on <a href="/wiki/Apartheid" title="Apartheid">Apartheid</a> in South Africa, condemning both the Apartheid Government and the <a href="/wiki/African_National_Congress" title="African National Congress">African National Congress</a> for committing atrocities.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/2004" title="2004">2004</a> – Representatives of the member states of the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a> signed the <b><a href="/wiki/Treaty_establishing_a_Constitution_for_Europe" title="Treaty establishing a Constitution for Europe">European Constitution</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, although it failed to be <a href="/wiki/Ratification" title="Ratification">ratified</a>.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1806" title="1806">1806</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Fourth_Coalition" title="War of the Fourth Coalition">War of the Fourth Coalition</a>: Believing they were massively outnumbered, the 5,300-man German garrison at Stettin, Prussia (now <a href="/wiki/Szczecin" title="Szczecin">Szczecin, Poland</a>), <b><a href="/wiki/Capitulation_of_Stettin" title="Capitulation of Stettin">surrendered</a></b> to a much smaller French force without a fight.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – Seventeen-year-old <a href="/wiki/Monarchy_of_Denmark" title="Monarchy of Denmark">Danish Prince</a> Vilhelm arrived in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a> to become <b><a href="/wiki/George_I_of_Greece" title="George I of Greece">George&#160;I</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_kings_of_Greece" title="List of kings of Greece">King of Greece</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1888" title="1888">1888</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Lobengula" title="Lobengula">Lobengula</a> of <a href="/wiki/Matabeleland" title="Matabeleland">Matabeleland</a> granted the <b><a href="/wiki/Rudd_Concession" title="Rudd Concession">Rudd Concession</a></b> to agents of <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Rhodes" title="Cecil Rhodes">Cecil Rhodes</a>, setting in motion the creation of the <a href="/wiki/British_South_Africa_Company" title="British South Africa Company">British South Africa Company</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1961" title="1961">1961</a> – The Soviet hydrogen bomb <b><a href="/wiki/Tsar_Bomba" title="Tsar Bomba">Tsar Bomba</a></b>, the largest <a href="/wiki/Nuclear_weapon" title="Nuclear weapon">nuclear weapon</a> ever detonated, was set off over <a href="/wiki/Novaya_Zemlya" title="Novaya Zemlya">Novaya Zemlya Island</a> in the Arctic Ocean as a test.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1991" title="1991">1991</a> – The <b><a href="/wiki/Madrid_Conference_of_1991" title="Madrid Conference of 1991">Madrid Conference</a></b>, an attempt by the international community to start a <a href="/wiki/Israeli%E2%80%93Palestinian_peace_process" title="Israeli–Palestinian peace process">peace process</a> through negotiations involving Israel and the <a href="/wiki/Arab_world" title="Arab world">Arab countries</a>, convened in <a href="/wiki/Madrid" title="Madrid">Madrid</a>.</li>
</ul>

<div style="float:right;margin-left:0.5em;" id="mp-otd-img">
<p><a href="/wiki/File:Martin_Luther,_1529.jpg" class="image" title="Martin Luther"><img alt="Martin Luther" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f8/Martin_Luther%2C_1529.jpg/93px-Martin_Luther%2C_1529.jpg" width="93" height="100" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f8/Martin_Luther%2C_1529.jpg/139px-Martin_Luther%2C_1529.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f8/Martin_Luther%2C_1529.jpg/186px-Martin_Luther%2C_1529.jpg 2x" data-file-width="600" data-file-height="645" /></a></p>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/475" title="475">475</a> – <b><a href="/wiki/Romulus_Augustulus" title="Romulus Augustulus">Romulus Augustulus</a></b> took the throne as the last ruling emperor of the <a href="/wiki/Western_Roman_Empire" title="Western Roman Empire">Western Roman Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1517" title="1517">1517</a> – According to traditional accounts, <b><a href="/wiki/Martin_Luther" title="Martin Luther">Martin Luther</a></b> <i>(pictured)</i> first posted his <b><a href="/wiki/The_Ninety-Five_Theses" title="The Ninety-Five Theses">Ninety-Five Theses</a></b> onto the door of the <a href="/wiki/All_Saints%27_Church,_Wittenberg" title="All Saints' Church, Wittenberg">Castle Church</a> in <a href="/wiki/Wittenberg" title="Wittenberg">Wittenberg</a>, present-day Germany, marking the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/Protestant_Reformation" title="Protestant Reformation">Protestant Reformation</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1822" title="1822">1822</a> – Emperor <b><a href="/wiki/Agust%C3%ADn_de_Iturbide" title="Agustín de Iturbide">Agustín de Iturbide</a></b> of the <a href="/wiki/First_Mexican_Empire" title="First Mexican Empire">First Mexican Empire</a> dissolved the <a href="/wiki/Congress_of_the_Union" title="Congress of the Union">Mexican Congress</a> and replaced it with a <a href="/wiki/Military_junta" title="Military junta" class="mw-redirect">military junta</a> answerable only to him.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1984" title="1984">1984</a> – <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_India" title="Prime Minister of India">Indian Prime Minister</a> <b><a href="/wiki/Indira_Gandhi" title="Indira Gandhi">Indira Gandhi</a></b> was <a href="/wiki/Assassination_of_Indira_Gandhi" title="Assassination of Indira Gandhi">assassinated</a> by two of her own <a href="/wiki/Sikhism" title="Sikhism">Sikh</a> bodyguards, sparking <b><a href="/wiki/1984_anti-Sikh_riots" title="1984 anti-Sikh riots">anti-Sikh riots</a></b> throughout the country.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a> – All 217 people on board <b><a href="/wiki/EgyptAir_Flight_990" title="EgyptAir Flight 990">EgyptAir Flight 990</a></b> were killed when the aircraft suddenly plummeted into the Atlantic Ocean off the coast of <a href="/wiki/Nantucket,_Massachusetts" title="Nantucket, Massachusetts" class="mw-redirect">Nantucket, Massachusetts</a>, United States.</li>
</ul>
