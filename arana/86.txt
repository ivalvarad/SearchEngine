<p><br />
Year <b>1740</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCXL</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Leap year starting on Friday">leap year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Leap year starting on Tuesday">leap year starting on Tuesday</a> of the 11-day slower <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1740&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_20" title="February 20">February 20</a> – The <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina_General_Assembly" title="North Carolina General Assembly">North Carolina General Assembly</a> incorporates the town of Newton as <a href="/wiki/Wilmington,_North_Carolina" title="Wilmington, North Carolina">Wilmington</a>, <a href="/wiki/Royal_Colony_of_North_Carolina" title="Royal Colony of North Carolina" class="mw-redirect">North Carolina</a>, named for <a href="/wiki/Spencer_Compton,_1st_Earl_of_Wilmington" title="Spencer Compton, 1st Earl of Wilmington">Spencer Compton, 1st Earl of Wilmington</a> and patron of Royal Governor <a href="/wiki/Gabriel_Johnston" title="Gabriel Johnston">Gabriel Johnston</a>.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_8" title="April 8">April 8</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Austrian_Succession" title="War of the Austrian Succession">War of the Austrian Succession</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">Royal Navy</a> captures the Spanish ship of the line <i><a href="/wiki/HMS_Princess_(1740)" title="HMS Princess (1740)">Princesa</a></i> off <a href="/wiki/Cape_Finisterre" title="Cape Finisterre">Cape Finisterre</a> and takes her into British service.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/June_26" title="June 26">June 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_Jenkins%27_Ear" title="War of Jenkins' Ear">War of Jenkins' Ear</a> – <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Fort_Mose" title="Siege of Fort Mose">Siege of Fort Mose</a>: A Spanish column of 300 regular troops, free black militia and Indian auxiliaries storms Britain's strategically crucial position of <a href="/wiki/Fort_Mose_Historic_State_Park" title="Fort Mose Historic State Park">Fort Mose</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a>.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_11" title="July 11">July 11</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pogrom" title="Pogrom">Pogrom</a>: <a href="/wiki/Jews" title="Jews">Jews</a> are expelled from <a href="/wiki/Little_Russia" title="Little Russia">Little Russia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_1" title="August 1">August 1</a> – The song <i><a href="/wiki/Rule,_Britannia!" title="Rule, Britannia!">Rule, Britannia!</a></i> is first performed at <a href="/wiki/Cliveden" title="Cliveden">Cliveden</a>, the country home of <a href="/wiki/Frederick,_Prince_of_Wales" title="Frederick, Prince of Wales">Frederick, Prince of Wales</a>, in England.<sup id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_17" title="August 17">August 17</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Benedict_XIV" title="Pope Benedict XIV">Pope Benedict XIV</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Clement_XII" title="Pope Clement XII">Pope Clement XII</a> as the 247th pope.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_9" title="October 9">October 9</a>–<a href="/wiki/October_22" title="October 22">22</a> – <a href="/wiki/1740_Batavia_massacre" title="1740 Batavia massacre">Batavia massacre</a>: Troops of the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_East_India_Company" title="Dutch East India Company">Dutch East India Company</a> massacre 5,000–10,000 <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Indonesians" title="Chinese Indonesians">Chinese Indonesians</a> in <a href="/wiki/Jakarta" title="Jakarta">Batavia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1740&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>By an act of the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Great_Britain" title="Parliament of Great Britain">Parliament of Great Britain</a>, alien immigrants (including <a href="/wiki/Huguenots" title="Huguenots" class="mw-redirect">Huguenots</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jew" title="Jew" class="mw-redirect">Jews</a>) in the colonies receive British <a href="/wiki/Nationality" title="Nationality">nationality</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Enfield,_North_Carolina" title="Enfield, North Carolina">Enfield, North Carolina</a>, is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Adam_Smith" title="Adam Smith">Adam Smith</a> enters <a href="/wiki/Balliol_College,_Oxford" title="Balliol College, Oxford">Balliol College, Oxford</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/George_Whitefield" title="George Whitefield">George Whitefield</a> founds the <a href="/wiki/Bethesda_Orphanage" title="Bethesda Orphanage" class="mw-redirect">Bethesda Orphanage</a> near <a href="/wiki/Savannah,_Georgia" title="Savannah, Georgia">Savannah, Georgia</a>.</li>
<li>Spain begins construction on <a href="/wiki/Fort_Matanzas" title="Fort Matanzas" class="mw-redirect">Fort Matanzas</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Matanzas_Inlet" title="Matanzas Inlet">Matanzas Inlet</a>, approximately 15 miles (24&#160;km) south of <a href="/wiki/St._Augustine,_Florida" title="St. Augustine, Florida">St. Augustine, Florida</a>.</li>
<li>Annual British iron production reaches 17,000 tons.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/University_of_Pennsylvania" title="University of Pennsylvania">University of Pennsylvania</a> is officially established.</li>
</ul>
