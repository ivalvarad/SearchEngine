<p><i><b>Portal</b></i> is a former <a href="/wiki/Television_program" title="Television program">TV show</a> that aired on <a href="/wiki/G4_(TV_channel)" title="G4 (TV channel)">G4</a>. During its two-season run from 2002–2004, it was one of the most popular shows on the network. Written, produced, and hosted by Dave Meinstein, <i>Portal</i> was in many ways a <a href="/wiki/Comedy" title="Comedy">comedy</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Soap_opera" title="Soap opera">soap opera</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Skit_comedy" title="Skit comedy" class="mw-redirect">skit comedy</a> show, and a news show. It was an attempt at a new type of show by blending <a href="/wiki/Satire" title="Satire">satire</a>, stylistic elements, and various levels of dramatism. <i>Portal</i> was the first television series to use <a href="/wiki/Machinima" title="Machinima">machinima</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-PC_Zone_2004.2C_12_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PC_Zone_2004.2C_12-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
