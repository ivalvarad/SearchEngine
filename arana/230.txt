<p><b>Portal</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Cochise_County,_Arizona" title="Cochise County, Arizona">Cochise County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arizona" title="Arizona">Arizona</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. It lies 25 miles (40&#160;km) south-southeast of <a href="/wiki/San_Simon,_Arizona" title="San Simon, Arizona">San Simon</a> and at the mouth of Cave Creek Canyon on the east side of the <a href="/wiki/Chiricahua_Mountains" title="Chiricahua Mountains">Chiricahua Mountains</a>. Often called the Yosemite of Arizona, the rock walled canyon is composed of fused volcanic tuff.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>The community is a popular location for birding in southeastern Arizona. It is also home to the <a href="/wiki/American_Museum_of_Natural_History" title="American Museum of Natural History">American Museum of Natural History</a> <a href="/w/index.php?title=Southwest_Research_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Southwest Research Station (page does not exist)">Southwest Research Station</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> The writer <a href="/wiki/Nancy_Farmer" title="Nancy Farmer">Nancy Farmer</a> also lives there currently with her husband.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Portal,_Arizona&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Typical of upland Arizona, Portal has a <a href="/wiki/Semi-arid_climate" title="Semi-arid climate">semi-arid climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen</a> <i>BSk</i>) with warm to hot days followed generally by quite cool nights. Frequent <a href="/wiki/Frost" title="Frost">frosts</a> in winter give way to mild days, whilst most rain comes from summer thunderstorms.</p>
