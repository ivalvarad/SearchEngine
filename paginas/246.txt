      Wikipedia, the free  					Jump navigation search        Portal of . portal is an opening in a wall of a building, gate or fortification, especially a grand entrance to an important   [ 1 ]   doors metal gates or portcullis in the opening can be used to control entry or exit. The surface surrounding the opening may be made of simple building materials or decorated with ornamentation The elements of a portal can include the voussoir tympanum an ornamented mullion or trumeau between doors, and columns with carvings of saints in the westwork of a church .
